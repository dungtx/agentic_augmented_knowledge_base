# Proposal framing — the UX pivot

## The problem

Previous delivery unit's showing focused too heavily on technical details and longer timeline. Customer questioned: "4 months for a basic OCR app for 1 supplier? Claude can read a bill already."

## The pivot

New proposal must lead with UX-first approach:
- Chat interface, simple upload, minimal data adjustment
- "Doesn't feel like a SaaS platform" — CDI's customer insight
- Technical complexity handled behind the scenes

## Key differentiators

1. **Corporate expenditure tracking** — attribute costs to specific vehicles, assets for better calculation. The longer the system runs, the more accurate the metrics.
2. **Dynamic AI-assisted dashboards** — differentiate from "hiring a data entry person with Claude access"
3. **Better than the baseline** — answering CDI's own question: why pay for this when you could hire a data entry person at $1/customer action?

## Narrative

"Our previous approach was technically sound but didn't match your customer's priority. Here's how we've adapted — UX-first, delivering value faster, with complexity hidden behind the interface."
