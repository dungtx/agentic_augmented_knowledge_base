# CDI-Cedar — ESG bill-reading platform

## Customer goal

CDI is an ESG SaaS vendor building a new platform for companies to track CO₂ emissions. They need an AI-powered bill-reading module as the core of this platform — users upload invoices/receipts, the system extracts data, maps it to ESG categories, and generates emissions reports and dashboards.

## Engagement

ITO engagement, ~30 man-months, 3 AI engineers. Proposal being reworked after a previous delivery unit's poor showing (over-indexed on technical details, missed customer's UX focus). Timeline: customer pushed back on 4 months, targeting 2–3 months for initial delivery. Phased approach planned but held back until after bid is landed.

## Technical landscape

Greenfield build — no existing constraints or data sources known. Scaling target: 3,000 companies, 3–5 users each, peak month-end auto-scaling. Per-declaration data isolation for integrity. Tech stack in consideration, no locks yet.

## People

One contact in Singapore (details TBD). Customer delegate visit to our office expected within 2 weeks. Internal: inherited from a delivery unit that focused on the wrong angle — need to be hands-on in meetings to course-correct.

## Key risks & unknowns

- OCR diversity — many bill types, only clear readable bills considered so far
- ESG domain knowledge gap — previous projects inaccessible from other delivery units
- Customer's customer base / business proportion — all conjecture from sales mining
- Timeline pressure (2–3 months vs scope breadth)
- MVP boundary unclear — many features discussed without prioritization
