# AI-Delivery-Pipeline — Research hub

A long-running research hub (not a bounded project) that exists to answer one question and
produce reusable intellectual core for the AIX service offering.

## Central question

> Can we formalize a delivery pipeline with AI at every step?

A well-shaped pipeline should, as a side effect, raise the baseline of the people using it. The
research focus is the pipeline itself; baseline-raising is the expected outcome of a good pipeline,
not a co-equal goal.
