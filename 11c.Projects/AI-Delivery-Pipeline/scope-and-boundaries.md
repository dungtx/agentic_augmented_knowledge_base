# Scope & boundaries — AI-Delivery-Pipeline

## Central question
Can we formalize a delivery pipeline with AI at every step?

A well-shaped pipeline should, as a side effect, raise the baseline of the people using it. The
research focus is the pipeline itself — baseline-raising is the *expected outcome* of a good
pipeline, not a co-equal goal.

## In scope
- SDLC (primary focus).
- Presales and ops — Dzung is a tech lead involved across all three.
- The pipeline formalization itself: prompting technique, context assembly, cost control &
  observation, how-much-context-is-enough, unknown-unknowns coverage, onboarding into the
  pipeline (active thread).

## Out of scope
- Personal AI routines — none currently exist in the vault; nothing to index.

## RIC seam (deliberately fuzzy)
This project is intended as the **reusable intellectual core**; [[../BJP26110-RIC/README|BJP26110-RIC]]
is a **case study** that applies it. The boundary between them is deliberately fuzzy at incubation —
knowledge needs to flow both ways until both incubate further. Forced premature separation is a
hazard; revisit the seam when the near-term artifact lands.

## What this is not
Not a client bid. Not a closeable project. Classification as "bid" status in frontmatter is a
kickoff-skill default; the real shape is a long-running research index.
