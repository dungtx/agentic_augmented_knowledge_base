I need to define a list of qualifications or general checks I can use to rate a candidate in an interview.

This is for a new hiring candidate for my department. The target role is AI engineer, but the rubric could also apply to a fullstack engineer.

Personal opinion: I care more about:
- Systems thinking
- Critical thinking
- Ability to express themselves
- Ability to go deep into a problem
- Adaptability
- Openness to new things

I dislike candidates who fully believe in AI and let AI take over their day-to-day work. I do not want people who rely on the technology instead of solving problems.

The checklist should help me evaluate whether the candidate thinks clearly, communicates clearly, can dig deeply into problems, and uses AI/tools as support rather than as a replacement for problem-solving.
