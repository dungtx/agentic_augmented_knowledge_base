Research on blanket materials for hot summer nights — needs to handle cold air (AC) during the night without being too warm.

## Key materials

| Material | Breathability | Warmth | Feel | Notes |
|----------|--------------|--------|------|-------|
| **Linen** | Excellent | Light | Cool, crisp, slightly rough | Best for hot sleepers. Highly breathable, wicks moisture, gets softer with washes. Keeps you cool but provides enough layer against AC chill. |
| **Cotton (percale weave)** | Very good | Light | Crisp, smooth | Percale = tight one-over-one weave, crisp and cool. Better for hot nights than sateen cotton (which is silkier but warmer). |
| **Bamboo / Tencel (lyocell)** | Very good | Light | Silky, cool-to-touch | Naturally temperature-regulating, moisture-wicking. Softer than cotton. Good for sensitive skin. |
| **Cotton (gauze / muslin)** | Excellent | Ultra-light | Soft, crinkled texture | Multiple thin layers create air pockets — insulates just enough against AC without trapping heat. Gets softer over time. |
| **Hemp** | Excellent | Light | Similar to linen, slightly rougher | Durable, antimicrobial, eco-friendly. Often blended with cotton for softness. |
| **Silk** | Good | Light-medium | Smooth, luxurious | Naturally temp-regulating but can feel warmer than linen/cotton. Good for skin/hair. Expensive. |

## Recommendation for Vietnam summer + AC

- **Top pick: Linen** — handles humidity well, breathable structure, lightweight layer that buffers AC draft without overheating.
- **Budget/soft pick: Cotton gauze/muslin** — multi-layer muslin blankets (like Aden + Anais style) are a sweet spot: breathable, light, soft, and the crinkled texture traps just enough air.
- **Avoid:** Polyester, microfiber, fleece, flannel — trap heat and don't breathe.

## Where to get in Vietnam
- Linen bedding: Home decor stores in HCMC/Hanoi, H&M Home, Zara Home, or Shopee/Lazada (Vietnamese linen brands exist — good quality)
- Cotton gauze blankets: Shopee/Lazada (search "chăn muslin" or "chăn cotton gạc"), baby stores carry muslin blankets
- Bamboo/Tencel: harder to find locally; Shopee/Lazada or international
