New bid opportunity — consultant position for RIC (same client as [[BJP26110-RIC Initial investigation into Skill marketplace]]).

**Customer goal:** learn our AI transformation technique to apply in their own company. They currently have some bottom-up understanding.

**Strategy from senior — consultant engagement flow:**

1. **Give them a top-down org chart** (like our AIX handbook) plus a **sidebar of AI integration components** mapped onto the org chart. Components include: governance, harness, observability, feedback loop, and others.

2. **Propose our engagement in stages:**
   - **Scout** — assess their current state (already happening)
   - **Evaluate** — give feedback on what exists
   - **Design** — architect the missing parts of their AI structure
   - **Run** — execute a sample project on the new system
   - **Operate** — run it alongside them
   - **Train & transfer** — hand over to their side

**Customer review focus:** They want to review based on these points, and possibly more:

- **Architecture review / design validity** — whether the proposed AI transformation architecture is coherent, feasible, and appropriate for their organization.
- **Security assessment** — whether the AI integration approach has adequate security controls, data boundaries, and risk management.
- **Guardrails and QA standards** — what standards, checks, and operational guardrails are needed to maintain quality when AI is used in delivery.
- **Accountability under full automation** — how responsibility, approval, auditability, and escalation should work when more of the workflow becomes automated.

**Additional discovery needed from senior:** We need to understand the customer's:

- **Target audience** — who inside RIC will use or be affected by the AI transformation model.
- **Intent** — what they actually want from the review: validation, learning, adoption roadmap, sales enablement, internal governance, or something else.
- **Current state** — what AI practices, tools, standards, teams, and governance already exist.

**Consultant proposal angle:** Frame our work as more than reviewing the visible artifacts. Use the discovery to identify missing AIX components, then propose either targeted consultant work or a broader service offering around the whole AI transformation system.

**Related:** a bottom-up/top-down AI transformation structure note will be seeded from lessons learned on this project. The AIX handbook is an internal asset under development — to be distilled into this repo later.
