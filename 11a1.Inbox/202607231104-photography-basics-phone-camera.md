# Photography basics with phone camera

Want to develop a general skill: take better photos by understanding and internalizing basic photography concepts, then practicing techniques with a phone camera. Areas to cover: composition (rule of thirds, leading lines, framing), lighting (natural light, golden hour, avoiding harsh shadows), exposure (what it is, how to adjust on phone), and practice drills to make the concepts stick so they become automatic.
