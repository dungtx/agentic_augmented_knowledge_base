# Story-Based Interview Answers

Story-based answers use a concrete episode to prove behavior rather than claim it.

Use STAR/CAR/PAR:

- Situation or context
- Task or challenge
- Action taken
- Result
- Reflection or lesson learned

This works best for behavioral questions, leadership, conflict resolution, and “tell me about a time…” prompts.

The story should not only recount events. It should expose the decision, tradeoff, and lesson that changed how I work.
