# Decision-Based Interview Answers

Decision-based answers emphasize why a choice was made, not just what happened.

Shape:

1. Name the decision.
2. List the options considered.
3. Compare constraints and tradeoffs.
4. Explain the chosen option.
5. Mention what would change the decision next time.

This is strong when interviewers care about judgment: architecture, senior engineering, product engineering, technical leadership, and design tradeoffs.

A good decision answer makes the interviewer believe I can choose under constraints.
