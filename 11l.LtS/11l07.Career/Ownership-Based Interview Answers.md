# Ownership-Based Interview Answers

Ownership-based answers make personal responsibility explicit in collaborative work.

Shape:

1. What did the team own?
2. What did I personally own?
3. Which decisions were mine?
4. What did others own?
5. What obstacles did I personally remove?

This is useful when projects were collaborative and the interviewer asks, “What exactly did you do?”

The answer should separate contribution from context without downplaying the team.
