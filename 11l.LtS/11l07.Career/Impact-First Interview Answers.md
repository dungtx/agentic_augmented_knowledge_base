# Impact-First Interview Answers

Impact-first answers start with the outcome before explaining the work.

Shape:

1. State the measurable result.
2. Explain the problem that made it matter.
3. Describe the key actions.
4. Tie the result back to users, team, or business.

Example frame: “I reduced deployment time from 45 minutes to 7 minutes by redesigning our CI pipeline.”

This is useful for accomplishments, résumé walkthroughs, and any answer where the interviewer needs a reason to care quickly.
