# Business-Outcome Interview Answers

Business-outcome answers connect engineering work to user or business value.

Shape:

1. Name the user or business problem.
2. Explain the technical change.
3. Quantify the result where possible.
4. Connect the result to customer experience, revenue, risk, cost, or delivery speed.

Example frame: “Checkout pages were timing out during peak traffic, affecting conversion. Query optimization reduced response time by 70% during sales events.”

This is useful when interviewing with product-minded teams or companies that expect engineers to understand impact.
