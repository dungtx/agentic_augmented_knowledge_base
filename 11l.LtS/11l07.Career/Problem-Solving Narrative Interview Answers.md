# Problem-Solving Narrative Interview Answers

A problem-solving narrative presents experience as an investigation.

Shape:

1. What was the problem?
2. Why was it difficult?
3. What hypotheses did I test?
4. What evidence changed my mind?
5. What solution followed from the evidence?

This is strongest for debugging stories, production incidents, performance problems, and ambiguous technical failures.

The signal is scientific thinking: I form hypotheses, verify assumptions, and let evidence redirect the solution.
