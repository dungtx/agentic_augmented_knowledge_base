# Design Review Style Interview Answers

Design review style answers present technical work like an internal engineering review.

Shape:

1. Requirements
2. Constraints
3. Alternatives
4. Final architecture
5. Risks
6. Future improvements

This is strongest for system design, architecture walkthroughs, senior interviews, and project deep dives.

The goal is to make the interviewer feel they are reviewing a well-reasoned design, not listening to a chronological story.
