# Principle-Based Interview Answers

Principle-based answers lead with an engineering philosophy, then use a short example to prove it.

Shape:

1. State the principle.
2. Break it into 2–3 operating rules.
3. Give a brief example.
4. Explain why this principle is repeatable.

Example frame: “When debugging distributed systems, I follow three principles: reproduce the issue, narrow the failure boundary, and verify assumptions with data.”

This works well for senior roles, system design, architecture discussions, and questions about how I think.
