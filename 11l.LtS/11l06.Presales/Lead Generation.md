# Lead Generation

Methods to identify and attract potential customers for ITO services. This is the **first stage** of the [[Sales Pipeline]].

## Channels

- **Cold mail** — Outbound email campaigns to target accounts
- **Leverage network / relations** — Referrals and existing contacts (highest conversion rate in ITO)
- **Seminar** — Thought-leadership and educational events (demonstrates technical credibility)
- **Multi-level rapport building** — Relationship building with staff at all levels within target organizations

## What Happens After Lead Generation?

Once a lead shows interest, they move to:
- **Qualification** → Assess fit (budget, authority, need, timeline)
- **Pitching** → First formal presentation or meeting

See [[Sales Pipeline]] for the full sequence.

## Notes

- Multi-level rapport (not just C-level) improves deal intelligence and reduces single-point-of-failure risk.
- In ITO, seminars and technical content marketing outperform cold outreach because buyers need to assess technical depth before engaging.
- Referrals from existing clients are the highest-quality leads — invest in delivery quality to generate them.
