# Customer Persona

The ideal buyer profile for ITO presales targeting. In practice, most deals involve a **buying committee** with multiple personas.

## Primary Personas

### The Economic Buyer (VP/C-Level)
- **Role**: Has budget authority, signs the contract
- **Motivation**: ROI, risk reduction, strategic alignment
- **Fears**: Project failure, budget overrun, reputational damage
- **Questions**: "Why should I spend this money?" "What's the business case?"

### The Technical Buyer (CTO, VP Engineering, Architect)
- **Role**: Evaluates technical fit and delivery capability
- **Motivation**: Technical quality, team competence, modern practices
- **Fears**: Poor code quality, communication gaps, technical debt
- **Questions**: "Can they actually build this?" "Will I regret this decision?"

### The User Buyer (Engineering Manager, Team Lead)
- **Role**: Will work directly with the ITO team
- **Motivation**: Team productivity, reduced workload, knowledge transfer
- **Fears**: Cultural/language barriers, timezone friction, "outsider" dynamics
- **Questions**: "Will this make my life easier or harder?"

## Common Traits Across Personas

- **Business mindset** — Even technical buyers need to justify decisions with business outcomes
- **Risk-averse** — ITO decisions are hard to reverse; buyers prefer proven over novel
- **Reference-dependent** — Case studies and client references carry significant weight

## Implications for Presales

| Persona | Lead With | Avoid |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Economic Buyer | Business case, ROI, risk mitigation | Deep technical architecture |
| Technical Buyer | Technical depth, process, quality assurance | Over-promising, vague answers |
| User Buyer | Communication plan, collaboration model, onboarding | Ignoring their concerns or treating them as invisible |

## Notes

- In ITO, the User Buyer often has veto power even without formal authority — if the team resists, the engagement fails.
- Multiple personas require multiple parallel conversations, not one presentation that tries to cover everyone.
