# Presale Steps

Formal stages and documents in the **Presale Validation** phase of the [[Sales Pipeline]]. This is where technical and commercial validation happens before contract.

## Activities

| Step | What It Is | Who Drives |
|------|-----------|------------|
| **Presentation** | Introduce company, capabilities, case studies | Vendor |
| **RFI** (Request for Information) | Customer gathers early info from multiple vendors | Customer |
| **RFP** (Request for Proposal) | Formal proposal invitation with detailed requirements | Customer |
| **Proposal** | Vendor's detailed response to RFP | Vendor |
| **RFQ** (Request for Quotation) | Price and commercial terms request | Customer |
| **WBS** (Work Breakdown Structure) | Detailed effort breakdown to justify cost | Vendor |
| **Negotiation** | Terms, pricing, and scope refinement | Both |
| **Contract** | Legal agreement and SOW finalization | Both |

## Sequence

```
Presentation → RFI → RFP → Proposal → RFQ/WBS → Negotiation → Contract
                ↓                                    ↓
           (shortlist)                        ( finalist )
```

## Relationship to Sales Pipeline

- **Presale Steps** = detailed view of the **Presale Validation** stage in [[Sales Pipeline]]
- After Contract → move to **Development** → **Delivery** → **Upsale**

## ITO-Specific Considerations

- **WBS** is critical in ITO RFQs — customers need transparency on effort, roles, and timeline to compare offshore/nearshore vs onshore options.
- **Pricing models** commonly used: fixed-price, time-and-materials, dedicated team (monthly rate), or hybrid.
- **Timezone and overlap hours** are often evaluation criteria in RFPs for offshore ITO.
- Each step is a gate: poor presentation → no RFI invitation; weak proposal → lose to competitor; unclear WBS → pricing distrust.

## Decision Points

- After RFI: Should we invest in the full RFP response? (Qualify the opportunity)
- After Proposal: Is the deal winnable at acceptable margin?
- After RFQ: Can we negotiate terms that protect margin while winning?
