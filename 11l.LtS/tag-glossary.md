# Tag glossary

> Controlled vocabulary for tags across all notes. When assigning tags during seed-inbox or distill-permanent, prefer terms from this list. Standardize: **lowercase, kebab-case, singular noun** unless the word only exists in plural (e.g. `career` not `careers`). Add new terms when a note genuinely introduces a new category — this glossary catches up later.

## Conventions

- **Case:** lowercase, hyphens between words (`deal-qualification` not `deal_qualification` or `Deal Qualification`).
- **Number:** singular unless the concept is inherently plural (`interviews` as a domain, `demos` as a category of activity).
- **Granularity:** precise over broad. `deal-qualification` over `sales`. `prompt-engineering` over `ai`.
- **Budget:** 2–6 tags per note.
- **Noise:** drop words that carry no signal for retrieval — `notes`, `thoughts`, `ideas`, `misc`.
